# Record Closet
This project was sparked by the idea, "what if I could just upload to Spotify directly?"
As of 2019, Spotify no longer accepts direct submissions from artists.
It makes sense from a business perspective, but it just doesn't sit right with me.

In order to upload to Spotify, an artist must go through a distributing company.
So that brought the question to my mind, "what if I started my own?" Hence this project.

The goal of Record Closet is to be a completely free music distributor.
The project aims to provide music delivery to major platforms.
However, the project is still in the "idea" stage, and is far from ready to be developed.

Yes, it costs money to operate a distributer company.
But the recording, the songwriting, the royalties all *should* belong to the artist.
They should be allowed to do with their earnings what they want.
And if they choose not to donate, that is their decision.

Hopefully, this idea will be realized and does not fall into the depths of unfinished projects.
We will see what happens as the year continues.
